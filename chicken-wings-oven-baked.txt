
= Oven Baked Chicken Wings

.Ingredients
----
* 1/2 T flour
* 1 1/2 T baking powder
* 1T Paprika
* 1 1/2 t ground black pepper
* 1 1/2 T seasoned salt (we don't use this)
* 1 T garlic powder
* 1 T onion powder
* 1 1/2 t dry mustard
----

.Directions
----
* preheat over 250F
* put rack on cookie sheet
* place chicken wings not touching on rack
* cook at 250F for 30 minutes
* raise over temperature to 425F
* cook another 15 to 45 minutes (based on chicken wing size)
----
